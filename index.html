Investment thesis§ 1

  1. Ships whole systems, not snippets. Corpus ingestion to eval harness to deployed frontend — the coverage universe below is built end-to-end and most of it is live in production, deployed and monitored by the subject himself.
  2. Treats trust as an engineering problem. Retrieval with chunk-level citations, refusal behavior tested like a feature, eval suites built before the demo. In a market flooded with fluent-but-unverified AI, verification is the scarce asset.
  3. Reads the 10-K and writes the parser. Dual mandate in CS and quantitative finance means the tools reflect how analysts actually work — risk decomposition, regime context, covenant headroom — not how a demo looks.
